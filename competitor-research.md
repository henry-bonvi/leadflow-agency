# LeadFlow Competitor Research

## Direct Competitors (Lead Gen for Trades)

### 1. Lead Genera / Similar Agencies
- **Pricing:** Usually $0.50-2 per lead, or monthly retainers $500-2k/mo
- **Delivery:** Ongoing, drip-fed
- **Quality:** Variable, often recycled lists

### 2. LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Scrapers
- **Pricing:** ~$100/mo for Navigator + scraping tools
- **Delivery:** DIY
- **Quality:** Good for B2B, not trades

### 3. ZoomInfo / Apollo / Lusha
- **Pricing:** $500-1500/mo
- **Delivery:** Self-serve database
- **Quality:** Good but generic, not trade-specific
